KEATS Calc

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 15, 2025

KEATS Calc is an ER shift billing tracker that helps calculate time used and estimated shift value based on billing codes. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

KEATS Calc does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network communication of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only, using your device's built-in storage:

  • Billing code configurations and unit counts
  • Shift start and end times
  • Weekend/stat holiday toggle preference

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the app developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

KEATS Calc does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this policy will be reflected on this page.
